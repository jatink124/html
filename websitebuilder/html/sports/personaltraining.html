Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our personal training services

How often should I train with a personal trainer?

Most clients see best results training 2-3 times per week. However, this varies based on your goals, schedule, and budget. We'll determine the optimal frequency during your initial consultation.

What should I bring to my first training session?

Bring comfortable workout clothes, athletic shoes, a water bottle, and a small towel. Your first session will include an assessment and a light workout to establish your baseline fitness level.

How quickly will I see results?

Most clients notice improvements in energy and strength within 2-3 weeks. Physical changes typically become visible around 4-6 weeks with consistent training and proper nutrition. Significant transformations usually take 8-12 weeks or longer.

Do you offer nutrition guidance?

Yes, nutrition is a crucial part of achieving your fitness goals. All training packages include some level of nutrition guidance, with more comprehensive nutrition coaching available as a standalone service or as part of our Elite package.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require 24 hours notice for cancellations. Sessions cancelled with less than 24 hours notice will be charged the full session fee. We understand emergencies happen, so each client receives one late cancellation exception per quarter.
