About Me

I'm Alex, a passionate UX Designer with 6+ years of experience

My journey in UX design has been driven by a curiosity about how people interact with technology and how design can enhance those interactions. I believe in creating intuitive, accessible, and delightful user experiences that solve real problems.

With experience working on projects across various industries including fintech, healthcare, e-commerce, and education, I bring a diverse perspective to my design work. I prioritize understanding user needs through research and testing, ensuring that my designs are not just visually appealing but also functional and user-centered.
