Frequently Asked Questions

What is the consultation process like?

Our consultation process begins with a brief initial discussion to understand your legal needs. We then match you with the attorney best suited to your case, followed by a detailed consultation where we analyze your situation and provide legal options. After the consultation, we outline a clear action plan and fee structure.

What are your fees and payment options?

Our fee structure varies depending on the type of legal service required. We offer hourly rates, flat fees, and contingency fees based on the nature of your case. We accept various payment methods including credit cards, bank transfers, and installment plans for certain services.

How quickly can I get an appointment?

For standard consultations, appointments are typically available within 2-3 business days. For urgent matters, we offer same-day emergency consultations based on availability. Online consultations can often be scheduled within 24 hours.

Can consultations be conducted online?

Yes, we offer secure video consultations through platforms like Zoom, Microsoft Teams, or our proprietary secure client portal. Online consultations provide the same legal expertise as in-person meetings with added convenience and accessibility.

What information should I prepare for my consultation?

To make the most of your consultation, please prepare a brief summary of your legal issue, any relevant dates or timeline of events, copies of pertinent documents, and a list of your questions or concerns. This will help us provide you with the most accurate advice during the initial consultation.
