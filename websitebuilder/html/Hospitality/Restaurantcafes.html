Our Story

About Savoria

Discover the passion and craftsmanship behind our culinary excellence.

A Culinary Journey Since 2005

Founded by renowned chef Michael Laurent, Savoria began as a small café in downtown with a vision to create a unique dining experience that combines traditional techniques with modern innovation.

Over the years, we have grown into a collection of fine dining establishments and cafés, each with its own character yet united by our commitment to culinary excellence and exceptional service.

Today, we continue to push the boundaries of taste and presentation, sourcing the finest ingredients from local farmers and artisans to create dishes that tell a story and evoke emotion.
