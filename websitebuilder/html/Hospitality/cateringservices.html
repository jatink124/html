Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our catering services

We recommend booking at least 4-6 weeks in advance for most events. For weddings and large corporate events, 2-3 months notice is preferable. However, we do understand that sometimes events are planned at short notice, so please contact us regardless and we'll do our best to accommodate you.

Absolutely! We can accommodate a wide range of dietary needs including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, dairy-free, and nut-free options. Please inform us of any allergies or dietary restrictions when booking, and our chefs will create appropriate alternatives.

Yes, all our packages include professional staff for food service. The number of staff depends on the size of your event and the package selected. For full-service events, we provide wait staff, bartenders, and a dedicated event manager.

Definitely! We encourage customization to make your event unique. After you book, we'll schedule a consultation to discuss your preferences, and our chef will work with you to create a personalized menu that suits your taste and budget.

We require a 50% deposit to secure your booking. This deposit is fully refundable if you cancel more than 30 days before the event. For cancellations within 14-30 days, 50% of the deposit is refundable. Cancellations less than 14 days before the event are non-refundable.
