Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our venues and services

We maintain an upscale dress code at all our venues. Smart casual attire is required at minimum. No athletic wear, ripped jeans, flip-flops, or overly casual attire. For specific venues like Mirage Nightclub, we recommend more formal attire, especially on weekends and for special events.

While walk-ins are welcome (subject to capacity), we strongly recommend making reservations, especially for weekends and special events. Reservations ensure you have a guaranteed entry and can be made through our online booking system or by calling our reservation line.

Our VIP memberships offer exclusive benefits across all our venues. Once you sign up, you'll receive a digital membership card in your email and a physical card within 7 business days. Membership benefits begin immediately upon confirmation of your subscription. You can upgrade or downgrade your membership level at any time with the changes taking effect at the start of the next billing cycle.

We accept all major credit cards, digital wallets (Apple Pay, Google Pay), and cash. For VIP services and bottle service, a credit card is required for reservation and minimum spend requirements apply. All prices shown on our website and menus include taxes.

Yes, all our venues are available for private events, from birthday celebrations to corporate gatherings. We offer customizable packages including catering, entertainment, decor, and more. Please contact our events team at events@pulse-nightlife.com to discuss your specific requirements and get a personalized quote.
