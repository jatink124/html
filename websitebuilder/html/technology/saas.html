Our mission and team

Our Story

Founded in 2018, techsaa began with a simple observation: businesses were wasting countless hours on repetitive tasks that could be automated. Our founders, seasoned technology experts with backgrounds in AI and enterprise software, set out to create a solution.

What started as a small project to help streamline workflows has grown into a comprehensive platform serving over 2,000 businesses worldwide. Our team has expanded from 5 passionate innovators to over 120 talented individuals committed to our mission.

Today, we're proud to be at the forefront of business process automation and intelligence, continually evolving our platform to meet the changing needs of modern organizations.
