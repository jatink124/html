Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to commonly asked questions about our hosting services.

What is web hosting? +

Web hosting is a service that allows individuals and organizations to make their websites accessible on the internet. When you purchase a hosting plan, you're essentially renting space on a server where your website files are stored and made available to visitors.

How do I transfer my existing website? +

We offer free website migration services for all new customers. Our technical team will transfer your website from your current host to our servers, ensuring a smooth transition with minimal downtime. Simply contact our support team to initiate the process.

Do you offer a money-back guarantee? +

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all our hosting plans. If you're not satisfied with our service within the first 30 days, you can request a full refund, no questions asked.

Can I upgrade my hosting plan later? +

Absolutely! You can upgrade your hosting plan at any time as your website grows. We'll simply prorate the difference between your current plan and the new one. The upgrade process is smooth and usually involves no downtime.

What kind of support do you offer? +

We provide 24/7 technical support through live chat, email, and phone. Our support team consists of experienced hosting specialists who can assist with any issues you might encounter, from general questions to complex technical problems.
