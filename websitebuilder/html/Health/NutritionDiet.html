Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our nutrition services

How does the personalized nutrition plan work?

Our nutrition plans are created based on your individual assessment results, including your goals, current health status, dietary preferences, and lifestyle factors. Our registered dietitians analyze this information to create a customized plan that includes meal recommendations, portion guidance, and nutrient timing strategies specifically for you.

How often are meals delivered?

Meal deliveries are typically made twice weekly (Monday and Thursday) to ensure maximum freshness. You can choose your preferred delivery schedule during signup. Our meals are delivered in eco-friendly, insulated packaging to maintain freshness even if you're not home at delivery time.

Can I customize my meal plan for allergies or preferences?

Absolutely! During your assessment, you'll have the opportunity to indicate any food allergies, intolerances, dislikes, or preferences. Our system automatically filters out unsuitable options, and your nutritionist will further customize your plan. We can accommodate most dietary restrictions including gluten-free, dairy-free, nut-free, vegetarian, vegan, and many others.

How often do I meet with my nutrition coach?

Coaching session frequency depends on your selected plan. The Essential plan doesn't include 1-on-1 coaching, the Premium plan includes two 30-minute sessions per month, and the Ultimate plan includes weekly sessions. All plans include unlimited messaging with our nutrition support team for quick questions between sessions.

Can I cancel or pause my subscription?

Yes, you can pause or cancel your subscription at any time through your account dashboard or by contacting our customer support team. For meal deliveries, we request at least 48 hours notice before your scheduled delivery date to process any changes.
