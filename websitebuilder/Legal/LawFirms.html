Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about legal processes and our services.

How much does a consultation cost?

We offer a complimentary initial consultation for all new clients. During this consultation, we'll discuss your case, answer your questions, and explain how we can help. If you decide to move forward, we'll discuss fee structures based on your specific legal needs.

How long will my case take?

The timeline for resolving a case varies widely depending on its complexity, the area of law, and current court backlogs. Some cases may resolve in weeks, while others might take months or even years. During your consultation, we'll provide a more specific timeline based on your unique situation and keep you updated throughout the process.

What documents should I bring to my first meeting?

For your initial consultation, please bring any documents relevant to your case. This may include contracts, police reports, medical records, correspondence, court documents, and any other paperwork related to your legal matter. Having these documents available helps us better understand your situation and provide more accurate advice.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we understand that legal expenses can be a concern for many clients. We offer flexible payment options and can work with you to develop a payment plan that accommodates your financial situation. For certain types of cases, such as personal injury claims, we work on a contingency basis, meaning you don't pay unless we win your case.

Can I switch lawyers if I'm not satisfied with my current one?

Yes, you have the right to change attorneys at any point during your case. If you're considering switching to our firm, we'll work to make the transition as smooth as possible. We'll request your case files from your previous attorney and quickly get up to speed on your matter. We pride ourselves on responsive communication and personalized attention to address any concerns you may have had with previous representation.

Still have questions? Contact our team for assistance.
